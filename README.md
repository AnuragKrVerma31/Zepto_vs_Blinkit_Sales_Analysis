# 🛒 Grocery Store War – Zepto vs Blinkit  
### 📊 Power BI Sales & Performance Dashboard
---

## 📌 Project Overview

**Grocery Store War – Zepto vs Blinkit** is an interactive **Power BI dashboard** designed to compare and analyze the performance of two leading quick-commerce platforms.

This dashboard provides actionable insights into:

- 📈 Sales performance  
- 🚚 Delivery efficiency  
- ⭐ Customer satisfaction  
- 🛍️ Product category trends  
- 💳 Payment mode preferences  
- 🌍 Geographic sales distribution  

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

To analyze and compare the operational and sales performance of Zepto and Blinkit in order to:

- Identify top-performing categories  
- Track monthly & quarterly sales trends  
- Understand customer payment behavior  
- Evaluate delivery time efficiency  
- Measure customer satisfaction through ratings

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Download the `.pbix` file.
2. Open it using **Power BI Desktop**.
3. Use filters (Quarter, Category, Brand, Payment Mode) to explore insights.
4. Interact with charts for deeper analysis.

---

## 📌 Key Insights Generated

- Paneer and Rice are among the highest-selling products.
- Sales peak during mid-year months.
- UPI & Card payments dominate over COD.
- Delivery time consistency impacts customer rating.
